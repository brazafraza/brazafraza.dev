Beeranged

Upgrade your player by defeating bees and levelling up. Pick the upgrades that suit you! Can you make it to the end and defeat the queen bee?

This game was a test project to see how quickly I could make a game with a simple addicting core loop, enemy AI, enemy scaling and upgrade systems. It was also a test of balancing numbers, seeing how I could make the player and enemies feel balanced, without the game feeling stale. Overall, I'm very happy with the outcome of the project and was able to advanced my c# skills :)
